Research Interests

Fracture Mechanics in elastic and viscoelastic materials

Understanding various crack propagations in different materials such as aluminum, concrete, and asphalt. interested in moving to more 3-D printed synthetics to replace bridges, houses, and the aero-industry analyzing fracture reliability and fatigue transitions

Finite Element Analysis

Using FEA softwares such as ANSYS, SCHISM to model various static and dynamics real world problems. A specific interest is the importance of natural frequency, resonance and damping effects effects on structures given certain geographical locations or applications such as stress concentrations and vibrations

Computational Fluid Dynamics

Analyzing various techniques to model different forms of heat transfer and pressure wave models. Additionally creating safety parameters by simulating various conditions to optimize a given task such as drag or flow of fluids
